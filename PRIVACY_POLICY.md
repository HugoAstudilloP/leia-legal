## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

When you use Leia, we collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

**Account Information:**
- Email address (used for authentication and account recovery)
- Display name (optional, set by you)
- User ID (automatically generated for your account)

**Pet Information:**
- Pet name, species, breed, sex, and birthdate
- Pet photos (stored securely and only visible to you)
- Physical characteristics (color, size, weight history)

**Health & Medical Records:**
- Vaccination records (vaccine name, date, veterinarian, notes)
- Deworming records (product name, date, dosage, notes)
- Weight measurements and history
- Medical events (consultations, surgeries, treatments)
- Allergy information
- Veterinary clinic information (name, contact)

**User Preferences:**
- Notification settings
- App theme preferences
- Onboarding completion status

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect certain information when you use the App:

**Usage Data:**
- Features used and frequency of use
- App screens visited
- Actions performed (pet created, vaccine added, etc.)
- Session duration and frequency
- Device information (model, operating system version)
- App version and build number

**Analytics Data:**
We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App. This includes:
- User engagement metrics
- Feature adoption rates
- User retention statistics
- Crash and error reports

**Device & Technical Data:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system and version
- App version
- Device language and region settings
- Push notification tokens (for reminder functionality)
- IP address (anonymized)

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:
- Precise geolocation data
- Contact lists or phone numbers
- Financial information or payment details
- Microphone or camera access (except when you choose to take photos)
- Social media credentials or friend lists
- Biometric data

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where We Store Data

Your data is stored using Firebase services, which are hosted on Google Cloud Platform infrastructure. Data may be processed and stored in:
- United States
- European Union
- Other regions where Google operates data centers

Firebase complies with major international data protection standards including GDPR, CCPA, and SOC 2.

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

**Technical Safeguards:**
- **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL
- **Encryption at Rest:** Data stored in Firebase is encrypted at rest
- **Firestore Security Rules:** Access controls ensure users can only access their own data
- **Authentication:** Firebase Authentication with secure session management
- **Access Controls:** Limited employee access to production data

**Operational Safeguards:**
- Regular security audits and updates
- Monitoring for suspicious activities
- Incident response procedures
- Data backup and recovery systems

**User Controls:**
- Password protection for accounts
- Email verification
- Account deletion capabilities

### 4.3 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specific retention periods:

- **Active Accounts:** Data retained indefinitely while account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
- **Analytics Data:** Aggregated, anonymized analytics retained for up to 24 months
- **Crash Logs:** Retained for 90 days for debugging purposes

You can request deletion of your account and data at any time through the App settings or by contacting support@leialabs.io.
